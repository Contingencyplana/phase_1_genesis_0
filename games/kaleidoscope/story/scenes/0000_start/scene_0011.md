# Scene: scene_0011

## Title
The Old Captain

## Location
Harbor Docks

---

## Scene Text

Near the far end of the docks, an older captain kneels beside a small sailboat, tightening a worn line around a wooden cleat.

His hands move with practiced care. His coat is sun-faded, and his voice is steady when he glances up to greet you.

"I still take this boat out now and then," he says. "Mostly short runs to a nearby island where some of my family live."

He wipes his hands on a cloth and studies the sky over the harbor.

"If the weather holds, I may go again soon. Could use another pair of hands for a simple trip."
