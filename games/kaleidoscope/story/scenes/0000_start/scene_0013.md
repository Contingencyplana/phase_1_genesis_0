# Scene: scene_0013

## Title
One More Pair of Hands

## Location
Harbor Docks

---

## Scene Text

The old captain gives a small, tired smile.

"Truth is, I am not as young as I used to be," he says. "Handling this boat alone gets harder every season."

He taps the hull with his knuckles.

"Nothing dramatic. Just easier with someone to help trim sail, steady cargo, and keep things moving when the wind shifts."

He nods toward a stack of empty crates.

"Sometimes I bring back fish. Sometimes supplies for family. Either way, one extra set of hands matters."
