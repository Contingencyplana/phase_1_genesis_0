## Scene Text

You make your way back down to the harbor docks.

The sun now sits higher over the bay, casting sharp light across wet planks and coiled ropes. Workers move between ships and stacked cargo while gulls wheel overhead, crying out over the noise of trade.

This is no first arrival now. The harbor feels familiar, busy, and alive with the island's daily rhythm.

From here, the village road climbs inland, with paths beyond it leading toward the library hill and the forest edge.
