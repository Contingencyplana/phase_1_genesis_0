# Scene: scene_0014

## Title
Tomorrow at First Light

## Location
Harbor Docks

---

## Scene Text

You tell him you may be willing to help.

The captain studies you for a moment, then gives a calm nod.

"Fair enough," he says. "If you decide to come, meet me here at first light. We'll keep it simple and stay with good weather."

He returns to his work, tightening another line along the rail.

The offer hangs quietly in your mind: not a departure yet, but a road opening beyond Harbor Island.
