# Scene: scene_0012

## Title
A Nearby Island

## Location
Harbor Docks

---

## Scene Text

The captain nods toward the open water beyond the harbor mouth.

"Not far," he says. "A nearby island. Some of my family settled there years back, and I still visit when I can."

He checks the knot he just tied, then gives it a firm pull.

"On a good day, the water is manageable. Still, the sea always deserves respect, even on a short crossing."

He looks out over the bay where smaller boats drift between distant points of land.
