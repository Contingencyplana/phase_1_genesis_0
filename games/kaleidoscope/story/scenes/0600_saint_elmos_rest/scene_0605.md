## Title
The Bell Post

## Location
Bell Post

---

## Scene Text

The bell hangs from a weathered timber frame bolted deep into the rock.

Green-black corrosion mottles the metal, but the rope is new enough to grip and ring.

The captain gives the line a short pull. The bell answers with a low note that rolls out across water and fog.

"In thick weather," he says, "this sound keeps boats from guessing where shore begins."

A second pull. Another slow tone.

No ceremony, no speech.

Just a signal, passed from one crossing to the next.
