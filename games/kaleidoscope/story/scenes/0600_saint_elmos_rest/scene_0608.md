# Scene: scene_0608

## Title
Crossing Back in the Evening Light

## Location
Storm Jetty

---

## Scene Text

By late day, the sky has flattened to a hard gray sheet beyond the island.

The captain checks the moorings, watches the chop, and nods toward the boat.

"Time to go before this line settles in," he says.

You cast off from Saint Elmo's Rest as the bell post fades behind spray and dusk light.

The crossing home stays manageable, but only just.

When Harbor Island's docks come into view, the captain eases his shoulders for the first time since departure.

"Places like that keep people alive," he says quietly. "Even when nobody agrees on the reasons."
