## Scene Text

Inside, the refuge house smells of damp wool, lamp oil, and old tea.

Long benches run along both walls. Folded blankets are stacked in uneven piles under a shelf lined with chipped cups and weather-marked tins.

Hooks by the door hold borrowed coats, spare rope, and a lantern with smoke-black glass.

A driftwood rack near the stove carries carved initials from crews who waited here through rough nights.

The room is plain, but every object feels placed by someone who needed shelter and left it ready for the next boat.
