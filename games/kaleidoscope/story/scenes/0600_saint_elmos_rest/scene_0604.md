# Scene: scene_0604

## Title
The Caretaker

## Location
Refuge House

---

## Scene Text

The caretaker is patching a torn sleeve at the end of one bench when you approach.

They look up, nod once, and set the needle aside.

"Boat make it in clean?" they ask.

When you say the crossing was manageable, they give a short, approving grunt.

"Good. This place isn't for heroics. It's for stopping early enough to keep people alive."

They gesture toward the door, where wind pushes at the frame.

"Sailors keep little habits here. Ring the bell in fog. Leave a token at the niche before a hard run."

A faint smile touches one corner of their mouth.

"Most days it's just tradition. Then the water turns, and tradition starts sounding practical again."
