## Scene Text

The captain brings the boat alongside a narrow jetty built from heavy timbers blackened by years of spray and weather.

Mooring ropes stand stiff with salt. Wet boards flex under your boots with a tired creak.

Beyond the jetty, a low refuge house sits behind a rough windbreak wall. A bell post leans near the edge of the path, its metal dark with age.

Nothing here asks you to linger.

Everything here is built to endure one more storm.
