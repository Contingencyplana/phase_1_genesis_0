# Scene: scene_0607

## Title
The Resting Stone

## Location
Wind Path

---

## Scene Text

Farther up the path, a broad stone sits half-buried where the wind breaks around the ridge.

Salt and lichen have claimed most of its surface.

When you brush one edge clear, a faint carved mark emerges.

A circle.

Inside it, a single dot.

The caretaker notices where you're looking.

"That old thing was here before the refuge house, before the bell frame, maybe before anyone kept records at all," they say.

They tap the stone once with their knuckles, then pull their coat tight against the rising gusts.

"People rest a hand on it before they head back down. Couldn't tell you why. They just do."

Clouds thicken offshore.
