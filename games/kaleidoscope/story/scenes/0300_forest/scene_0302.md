# Scene: scene_0302

## Title
The Old Signpost

## Location
Forest Edge

---

## Scene Text

You step closer to the weathered signpost standing beside the forest path.

The wood is gray with age, its surface cracked by years of sun, wind, and rain. One of the arms hangs slightly crooked, as if it has been struck or pushed aside long ago.

You brush away a layer of moss.

Faint lettering emerges beneath the dirt.

One arrow points deeper into the forest. The carved words are difficult to read, but a few letters remain visible.

**...Old Stones**

Another arrow points toward a narrow trail branching away from the main path.

This one is even harder to read.

**...Circle**

Beneath both arrows, someone has scratched a smaller marking into the wood.

A circle.

And inside it, a single dot.

The same symbol you saw in the library journals.

You step back and look toward the darker part of the forest where the trail continues.

The signpost may have been placed here long ago to guide travelers.

Or perhaps to warn them.

The forest waits quietly beyond the trees.
