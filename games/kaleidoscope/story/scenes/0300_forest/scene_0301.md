# Scene: scene_0301

## Title
Deeper into the Forest

## Location
Island Forest

---

## Scene Text

You follow the narrow trail deeper into the forest.

The sounds of the harbor fade quickly behind you. Soon the only noises are the quiet rustle of wind through the tall pines and the occasional call of distant birds.

The path twists between thick roots and moss-covered stones. Sunlight filters down through the branches in thin golden beams, illuminating patches of ferns and fallen needles.

After a short distance, the forest begins to feel older.

The trees grow closer together here, their trunks wider and their bark deeply scarred by time. The ground beneath your feet feels uneven, as though something lies buried beneath the soil.

You slow your steps.

Ahead, something catches your eye.

Near the edge of the trail, a portion of smooth stone protrudes from the earth. Unlike the rough rocks scattered across the forest floor, this stone surface is strangely flat.

You brush aside a layer of moss.

Beneath it, faint markings appear.

A circle.

And inside it, a single small dot.

The same symbol you saw in the library.

You glance deeper into the forest.

If the Visionary truly followed these signs, then this trail may lead to something far older than the harbor village.

The forest around you stands silent.

Waiting.

You take one last look at the strange marking in the stone before stepping back toward the forest trail.
