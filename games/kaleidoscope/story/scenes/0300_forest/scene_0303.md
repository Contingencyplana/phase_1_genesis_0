## Title
Old Stones

## Location
Deep Forest

## Scene Text

You follow the trail deeper into the forest.

The trees grow thicker here, their branches weaving together to dim the sunlight. The air feels cooler, and the sounds of the harbor have long since faded away.

After several minutes the path opens into a small clearing.

Broken stones lie scattered across the ground, half buried beneath moss and fallen leaves.

Whatever once stood here has long since collapsed.

Yet the arrangement of the stones feels deliberate.

Someone built something here.

And someone abandoned it.
