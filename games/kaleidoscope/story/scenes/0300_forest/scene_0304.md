# Scene: scene_0304

## Title
The Stone Circle

## Location
Deep Forest

---

## Scene Text

You step carefully through the clearing, brushing aside moss and fallen leaves.

At first the stones appear scattered at random. But as you walk slowly through the clearing, the pattern begins to emerge.

They were once arranged in a circle.

Several taller stones still stand upright while others have collapsed and sunk into the soil over the centuries. Moss and vines cling to their surfaces.

You kneel beside one of the remaining pillars and brush away a patch of moss.

Beneath it, the familiar marking appears again.

A circle.

And within it, a single dot.

You look around the clearing more carefully now.

The stones are positioned with surprising precision, forming a wide ring around the center of the clearing.

At the very center lies a flat stone slab, partially buried beneath layers of earth and fallen needles.

You clear the surface with your hand.

The same symbol has been carved deeply into the stone.

The marking resembles the strange symbol recorded in the island library journals.

For a moment the forest grows strangely quiet.

The wind stills.

Even the birds seem to have fallen silent.

You suddenly understand why the Visionary came here.

This place was never meant to be seen from the harbor.

It was meant to be found.
