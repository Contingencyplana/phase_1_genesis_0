# Scene: scene_0708

## Title
Rumor of the Old Mooring Stone

## Location
Edge of Tidemarket / Older Waterfront Margin

---

## Scene Text

The newer planks thin out along the margin where older stonework still shows between patched retaining walls.

A small knot of locals is gathered near a roped section of quay, speaking in low, heated turns.

"It's one stone," a younger worker says. "Move it, square the line, and open room for two more berths."

An older local with tide-cracked hands shakes her head.

"Stone-minded," someone mutters about her, half mockery and half respect.

She ignores it.

"That marker held before your father's father tied here," she says. "You don't shift everything old just because traffic got louder."

Another voice answers, "Then at least let the survey crew read it properly before someone builds over it by accident."

Preserve it. Study it. Move it. Build past it.

The disagreement is practical on the surface, but the caution underneath is unmistakable.

Here, the older mystery is no longer hidden out in forest stones or distant ruins.

It is inside civic expansion itself.
