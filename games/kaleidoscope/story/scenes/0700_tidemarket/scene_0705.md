# Scene: scene_0705

## Title
The Notice Board

## Location
Wharf Office / Route Board

---

## Scene Text

The notice board is larger than a door and layered with pinned sheets, grease-pencil marks, weather cautions, and route revisions.

Some notes are fresh and crisp.
Others are damp at the corners and crossed through by hand.

Departure windows.

Channel restrictions.

Cargo limits.

A warning about driftwood choke in an inner cut.

A clerk's note about rerouting three ferries by lantern signal.

You realize quickly that in Tidemarket, information moves cargo as much as rope and muscle do.

A younger assistant taps one column with the back of a pen.

"These are only the short entries," she says. "Full records and dispute copies are held at the house inland."

"Ledger House?" someone nearby asks.

She nods without ceremony and keeps sorting papers.

Behind the noise of the quay, you can feel a larger coordinating structure at work.
