# Scene: scene_0502

## Title
Landing Pier

## Location
Lantern Key Pier

---

## Scene Text

The captain eases the boat against a narrow pier bolted into black stone.

Lantern Key is smaller than Brinehook and far more exposed. Wind moves through everything: lines, jackets, loose boards, hanging hooks.

Oil casks sit under tarps weighted with rope. A rack of signal flags snaps hard against a post. Someone has chalked the day's wind direction on a slate near the pier steps.

Nothing here feels decorative.

Everything feels maintained.

Above, a path climbs toward a lantern tower set on the island's highest shoulder.
