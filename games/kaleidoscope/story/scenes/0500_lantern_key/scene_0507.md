# Scene: scene_0507

## Title
The Old Foundation Stone

## Location
Rock Shelf Below the Tower

---

## Scene Text

The shelf below the tower holds a block of older stone half-set into the rock.

Its edges do not match the newer masonry above. Wind and grit have worn most of its face smooth.

When you brush away salt dust, a shallow mark appears.

A circle.

Inside it, a faint dot almost erased by time.

The keeper's steps crunch on gravel behind you.

"Tower crew found that when they repaired the footings years back," they say. "Older than our records. Older than anyone's stories, really."

They rest a hand on the stone, practical as ever.

"We built on what was here. That's usually how islands work."

The wind rises again, carrying the smell of oil and rain.
