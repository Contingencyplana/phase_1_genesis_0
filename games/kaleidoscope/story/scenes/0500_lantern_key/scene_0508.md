# Scene: scene_0508

## Title
Return Before Nightfall

## Location
Lantern Key Pier

---

## Scene Text

By late afternoon the keeper has already started lantern checks for evening.

The old captain secures a small bundle of pier supplies and watches the sky for a clean return window.

"Best leave before the next line of weather," he says.

You cast off into a steady crosswind. Lantern Key slips behind you: tower, yard, and the hard-working pier shrinking against the horizon.

The run back to Harbor Island is ordinary sail, ordinary weather, and practiced hands.

Another island in the chain.

Not distant, just necessary.
