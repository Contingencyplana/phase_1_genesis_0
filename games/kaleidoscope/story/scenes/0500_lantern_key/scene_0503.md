## Scene Text

The beacon yard sits in a shallow cut of rock beside the tower path.

Oil casks are lined in rows by date marks. Coils of tarred rope hang from iron pegs. A workbench holds weathered tools, spare lenses, and signal fittings wrapped in cloth.

A brass horn, dented but polished, rests beside a stack of lantern chimneys.

Every surface shows use.

This is not a place for stories. It is a place that keeps ships from breaking in the dark.

A lone keeper checks a ledger board, then tightens a strap over a crate before the wind can take it.
