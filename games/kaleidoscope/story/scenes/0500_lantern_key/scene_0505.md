## Scene Text

The path climbs in narrow turns between stone outcrops worn smooth by salt wind.

Grass grows in low patches, bent nearly flat. Iron handposts have been driven into the rock where footing gets slick.

Below, the pier looks small against open water.

Up here the island feels lonelier than Brinehook, less sheltered, more watch post than home.

The tower stands ahead with its lantern glass catching pale light. Just below it, an older shelf of rock breaks away from the path.
