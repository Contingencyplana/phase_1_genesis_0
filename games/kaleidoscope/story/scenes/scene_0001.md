# Scene: scene_0001

## Title
Arrival at Harbor Island

## Location
Harbor Village

---

## Scene Text

Your ship arrives at Harbor Island just after dawn.

The harbor is already awake. Sailors shout across the docks while merchants unload crates from a recently arrived vessel. The smell of salt and tar hangs in the morning air.

Beyond the harbor village, a narrow path climbs toward an old stone library overlooking the sea. Another path disappears into the forest beyond the village.

You step onto the dock, unsure what you will find on this island — only that many strange stories seem to begin here.
