# Scene: scene_0203

## Title
View from the Library Hill

## Location
Library Hill

---

## Scene Text

You step away from the library entrance and walk toward the edge of the hill.

From here the entire harbor spreads out below you.

Ships drift slowly in the morning tide while dockworkers move like tiny figures between stacks of cargo. Gulls circle lazily above the water, their cries carried upward by the sea wind.

The harbor village feels small from this height — a cluster of wooden buildings gathered along the shoreline.

Beyond the village, the forest stretches across much of the island. From here the trees form a dark green sea, their canopy shifting gently in the wind. Somewhere within those woods, the narrow trail you saw earlier disappears from sight.

Farther out, the open ocean glitters beneath the rising sun.

For a moment the island feels peaceful.

And yet…

Standing here beside the old library, it is easy to imagine that many people have stood in this same place before you — watching the harbor, studying the island, wondering what secrets might still lie hidden beyond the village.

The wind rustles the grass around the hilltop.

Behind you, the library door stands quietly open.
