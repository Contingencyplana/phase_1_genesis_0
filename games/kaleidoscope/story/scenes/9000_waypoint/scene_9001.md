# Scene: scene_9001

## Title
Waypoint Island

## Location
Dream

---

## Scene Text

That night, after a long day exploring Harbor Island, you finally find a place to rest.

The harbor has grown quiet. Ships creak softly against their moorings, and the distant sound of waves drifts through the open window.

Sleep comes quickly.

---

You find yourself standing on a small island beneath a sky filled with unfamiliar stars.

The air is calm. The sea around the island glows faintly in the moonlight, stretching endlessly in every direction.

At the center of the island stands a single stone marker.

You walk toward it.

The stone is smooth and ancient, its surface marked by a familiar symbol.

A circle.

And within it, a single dot.

The same mark you saw carved into stone in the forest. The same symbol drawn in the Visionary's journals.

As you look closer, faint shapes begin to appear around the island.

Far across the water, other islands rise from the dark sea.

Some glow softly.

Others flash with distant lightning.

A few stand quiet and silent beneath the stars.

You sense that each island represents a moment in a much larger journey.

The stone marker before you hums softly, as though acknowledging your arrival.

You have reached a waypoint.

The sea wind stirs, and the stars above begin to shift.

---

You wake slowly as morning light fills the room.

For a moment the dream lingers in your thoughts — the island, the stone marker, and the distant shapes of other islands waiting somewhere beyond the horizon.

Then the sounds of Harbor Island return.

The day has begun.
