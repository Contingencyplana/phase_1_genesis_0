# Scene: scene_1203

## Title
Convergence at the Split Marker

## Location
Inland Route Split / Shelf Above the Storage Rise

---

## Scene Text

The path narrows, then opens on a shelf where two practical routes meet beneath a split marker post.

One branch carries goods back toward the civic shore.

The other runs inland along a line absent from newer public copies but present in older survey leaves.

A route tally board fixed under the post records both branches in plain numbers.

Recent carrier marks confirm regular movement on each.

There is no mystery performance here.

Only hard evidence that the continuation exists as working terrain, not rumor, and that the archive contradiction was pointing to a real travel system.

From this shelf, the archipelago feels newly measurable.

Not solved.

Reachable.
