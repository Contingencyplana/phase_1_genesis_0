# Scene: scene_1201

## Title
Inland Departure

## Location
Inland Archives / Departure Yard

---

## Scene Text

The workroom quiet does not follow you outside.

In the Departure Yard below, carriers are tightening straps, checking boot wraps, and balancing loads that have to survive stone, slope, and sudden rain.

A narrow board fixed to a post lists route names, older aliases, and weather cautions in different hands.

What looked like contradiction on the table resolves into procedure here: packet tags matched to path windows, ridge turns logged with times, and fallback shelters marked in plain ink.

No one speaks as if inland travel is an adventure.

They speak as if it is work that becomes dangerous when people pretend it is simple.

A copied route leaf from the archives sits folded in your hand.

Its endpoint is the same storage rise the civic map treats as a finish.

The older notation says it is only the first hinge.
