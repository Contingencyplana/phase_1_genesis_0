# Scene: scene_0104

## Title
Whispers of the Forest

## Location
Harbor Village

---

## Scene Text

The sailor glances toward the path that disappears into the trees beyond the village.

"The forest?" he says slowly. "Aye, there's stories about that place too."

He folds his arms and watches the distant tree line for a moment.

"Old paths run through those woods. Some of them older than the harbor itself."

He lowers his voice slightly.

"Travelers say strange things happen out there. Lights sometimes flicker between the trees at night. And once in a while someone comes back claiming they found ruins deeper in the forest."

The sailor shrugs, as if unsure whether he believes the tales himself.

"Could just be stories. But stories have a way of starting somewhere."

He nods toward the narrow trail that leads away from the village.

"If you're feeling curious, that's the way to find out."

The wind carries the scent of pine and earth from the distant woods.
