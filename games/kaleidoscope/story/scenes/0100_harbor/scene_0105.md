## Title
Harbor Market

## Location
Harbor Village

## Scene Text

A cluster of canvas stalls spreads along the edge of the harbor square.

Merchants call out to passing sailors while crates of fruit, tools, and strange curiosities are arranged across rough wooden tables. The air smells of salt, spices, and freshly baked bread.

A fisherman argues loudly over the price of a net while a woman carefully polishes a small brass compass at her stall.

Children weave between the tables chasing a gull that has stolen a scrap of bread.

Beyond the noise and motion of the market, the harbor water glitters in the morning sun.

It seems like the sort of place where a traveler might hear useful rumors… or discover something unexpected.
