# Scene: scene_0108

## Title
Strange Instruments

## Location
Harbor Market

---

## Scene Text

You gesture toward the unusual devices laid out across the merchant’s table.

The woman smiles slightly, as though she has been waiting for that question.

"Most travelers notice the compass," she says, tapping the polished brass instrument. "But these are the tools that interest explorers."

She carefully lifts a small metal frame fitted with thin glass lenses.

"Survey tools. Measuring devices. Instruments used to study land and sea."

She sets it down and picks up another curious object — a small circular plate etched with fine markings.

"Some came from scholars who passed through Harbor Island years ago. Others from ships that brought strange cargo from distant places."

Her eyes drift briefly toward the hill where the library stands.

"People who study the old journals often come looking for tools like these. The island seems to inspire that sort of curiosity."

She places the device back on the table.

"If you're planning to explore, understanding where you stand can matter just as much as knowing where you're going."

Around you, the market continues its steady hum of conversation and trade.
