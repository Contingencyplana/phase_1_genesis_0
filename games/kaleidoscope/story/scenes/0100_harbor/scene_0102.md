# Scene: scene_0102

## Title
Rumors of the Visionary

## Location
Harbor Village

---

## Scene Text

The sailor leans against a stack of cargo crates and lowers his voice.

"You’re not the first traveler to come here chasing strange stories," he says.

"People say this island used to be quiet. Just a harbor and a few fishermen."

He gestures toward the hill where the old library stands.

"But then those old journals started turning up. Plans. Ideas. Fragments of something bigger."

The sailor scratches his beard thoughtfully.

"Some say a visionary passed through here years ago. Someone who believed the world could be... different."

He glances toward the forest path.

"And some say that whatever they started here isn’t finished."

The harbor noise swells around you again as the sailor waits to see what you will do next.
