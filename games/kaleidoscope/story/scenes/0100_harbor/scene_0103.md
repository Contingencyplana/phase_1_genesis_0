# Scene: scene_0103

## Title
The Library on the Hill

## Location
Harbor Village

---

## Scene Text

The sailor glances up toward the hill where the old stone library stands against the morning sky.

"That place has been here longer than the harbor," he says. "Older than most of the village, if the stories are true."

He shifts his weight against the crates and lowers his voice slightly.

"Travelers come here looking for those journals. Strange writings about new ways of building things. New ways of thinking."

The sailor shrugs.

"Most people can't make much sense of them. But every now and then someone spends a few days up there reading… and leaves looking like they've seen the future."

He nods toward the winding path that climbs the hill.

"If you're curious, that's where I'd start."
