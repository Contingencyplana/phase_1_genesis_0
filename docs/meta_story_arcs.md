# Meta Story Arcs

Meta story arcs represent moments of progress in the evolution of the Kaleidoscope world.

Unlike normal story arcs, which describe locations and events within the world, meta arcs reflect the growth of the project itself.

These scenes appear rarely and are intended to feel special.

## Types of Meta Arcs

Kaleidoscope uses several types of meta arcs (dream arcs) to reflect the progress of the ecosystem and the discoveries made during development. These hidden roving islands are only visited for real at the end of stages.

### Waypoint (9000)

Waypoints represent small but meaningful moments along the journey.

They may mark the completion of a minor task, the discovery of an interesting idea, or the beginning of a new direction.

Waypoints help record the path of exploration without requiring a major accomplishment.

---

### Progress (9100)

Scenes that hint at the Island of Progress.

These occur occasionally during development and reflect gradual advancement.

They often appear as subtle narrative reflections of steady improvement within the ecosystem.

---

### Milestone (9200)

Scenes marking the completion of major stages of development.

At these moments, the Island of Progress may become a real location rather than a dream.

Milestones indicate that an important phase of the system has been successfully completed.

---

### Breakthrough (9300)

Scenes representing important discoveries or technological breakthroughs within the Kaleidoscope world.

These appear when major capabilities are unlocked or when the project reaches a new level of possibility.

---

### Achievement (9400)

Achievements represent especially significant accomplishments within the ecosystem.

They celebrate moments where the system demonstrates something remarkable or unexpected.

Achievements may highlight particularly meaningful discoveries, creations, or collaborative successes.

## Meta Arc Ranges

Meta story arcs occupy the reserved scene identifier range:

9000–9999

Initial meta-arc folders include:

9000_waypoint 
9100_progress 
9200_milestone 
9300_breakthrough 
9400_achievement  

These arcs represent different kinds of progress in the evolution of the Kaleidoscope world.

Additional meta arcs may be defined later if needed, but the total number should remain small to preserve clarity and avoid unnecessary complexity.

## Design Principles

Meta scenes should be:

- rare
- short
- symbolic

They exist to celebrate progress and to remind players that the world itself is evolving.
